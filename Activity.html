Join Us

We are building a global, paper-driven research community focused on AI and medical intelligence.

Who We Are Looking For

  • Undergraduate interns
  • Master’s student interns
  • Ph.D. student interns
  • Visiting students

We value motivation and commitment over background labels. Ideal candidates are interested in AI, enjoy sharing knowledge, and are willing to engage in publication-oriented research.

No prior publications are required. What matters is taking the process of producing papers seriously.

What We Do Together

Our goal is straightforward: to produce high-quality research for top-tier journals and conferences.

  • Study AI and related research directions
  • Identify and refine research problems
  • Complete the full pipeline: idea → method → experiment → paper
  • Advance academic and professional development through publications

Whether you have no clear idea or already have a strong one, we work together to turn it into a publishable contribution.

How We Work

We operate as a long-term online research collective with structured activities, including:

  • AI learning and knowledge sharing
  • Classic paper reading sessions
  • Frontier research briefings
  • Open group meetings focused on paper progress

Our single objective is to keep learning continuous and ensure steady paper output.

What You Will Gain

  • Exposure to the Key Laboratory of Brain Health Intelligent Evaluation and Intervention
  • Opportunities for graduate study, Ph.D. training, and visiting positions
  • Access to academic resources, mentorship, and research networks
  • Strong discipline driven by deadlines — the first productivity force
