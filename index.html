Welcome

I’m a Post-doctoral fellow at the Faculty of Science and Engineering, Waseda University, supervised by Prof. Liu Jiang. Previously, I received my PhD degree from Beijing Institute of Technology in June 2025. I am a member of the Key Laboratory of Brain Health Intelligent Evaluation and Intervention (BIT), Ministry of Education, P. R. China, supervised by Prof. Bin Hu (Fellow, IEEE), Prof. Kun Qian and Prof. QunXi Dong.

Welcome to my research page. We work at the intersection of medicine and engineering, focusing on healthcare applications of privacy-preserving computational artificial intelligence. Our mission is to develop trustworthy AI methods and models that safeguard sensitive medical data while advancing precision, data-driven healthcare.

Our research focuses on developing and applying trustworthy AI that enables the secure and efficient use of distributed medical data. We explore a range of topics in AI for healthcare, such as Federated Learning for Healthcare, Psychophysiology of Privacy Computing, and Privacy-based LLM/LMM for Healthcareb>, and beyond.
