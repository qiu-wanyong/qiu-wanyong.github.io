Welcome to TDSAI Lab!

The Trustworthy Data Science and AI (TDSAI) Lab, also known as Cao Lab, is at the forefront of advancements in trustworthy data science and AI, with a strong emphasis on privacy, security, reliability, fairness, and transparency. Our mission is to develop groundbreaking theories, algorithms, and systems that promote ethical and dependable AI, contributing to the welfare of society and the future of humanity.

Our current research focuses on developing and applying privacy-enhancing technologies to address critical challenges in data management, machine learning, and security. Key areas of exploration include Differential Privacy, Federated Learning, Trustworthy LLM/ML, AI Security, Data Marketplaces, and beyond.
